# GRE Resources

I had to take the GRE in the Fall of 2025. This repository contains some of resources I used and created to help me study for it.

## GRE Vocab

I created a spreadsheet with words that were the most difficult to me, and filled it out with etymologies and other helpful bits of information to aid retrieval.

Key of abbreviations:

- Lt - Latin
- Gk - Greek
- OE - Old English
- ME - Middle English
- En - English
- OF - Old French
- Fr - French
- Gr - German
- It - Italian
- cf - conferare "compare"
- sth - something
- qqc - quelque chose ("something" in French)
